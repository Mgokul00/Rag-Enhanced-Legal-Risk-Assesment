Proprietary Scoring

The Legal Risk Index

The LRI is a composite 0–100 risk score derived from four sub-signals per risk category: keyword density, RAG semantic similarity, threat density, and Gemini's severity assessment. Weighted and normalised across 9 legal risk dimensions, it produces a single defensible number your clients understand.

0–19 — Low risk. Proceed with standard checks.
20–44 — Medium risk. Additional verification recommended.
45–69 — High risk. Legal counsel required before proceeding.
70–100 — Critical. Do not proceed without full legal review.
